Study Hacks of the Week
Work smarter, not harder
01

Use the 50/10 rule instead of Pomodoro

Study for 50 minutes, break for 10. Your brain handles deeper work in longer blocks — Pomodoro's 25 mins barely scratches the surface.

02

Teach it out loud before the test

Explain a concept to yourself like you're the teacher. If you stumble, that's exactly what you don't know yet.

03

Highlight less, question more

Instead of highlighting, write one question per paragraph. Forces your brain to actually process information.
